The Lakeside Philosophy

Why is recruitment so often measured by the speed of hiring, rather than the long-term success of the people being hired?

Throughout my HR journey, one belief has remained constant — recruitment should never be reduced to filling vacancies. Every hiring decision shapes culture, leadership, business performance, and the future of an organization.

Lakeside Recruit was established to provide organizations with a more strategic approach to talent acquisition. Rather than simply sourcing candidates, we partner with businesses to understand their goals, culture, workforce priorities, and long-term vision before recommending talent that genuinely aligns with those objectives.

A Duality of Expertise

Over two decades of HR leadership have provided me with a deep understanding of talent strategy, organizational development, and executive hiring. Complementing this experience are my qualifications as a Certified Counsellor and Psychometric Assessment Professional, bringing an additional behavioural perspective to recruitment.

Organizations seeking deeper hiring insights may also choose to incorporate behavioural, psychometric, and role-fit assessments through the broader Lakeside ecosystem as an optional premium assessment service, providing an additional layer of confidence for leadership, specialist, and business-critical hiring decisions.
