Recruitment Beyond Resumes

Why is recruitment so often measured by the speed of hiring, rather than the long-term success of the people being hired?

Over two decades in Human Resources have reinforced one belief that exceptional hiring is never about filling vacancies quickly. Every hiring decision influences leadership, organizational culture, business performance, and long-term growth.

Lakeside Recruit was established to bring a more strategic approach to talent acquisition. Partnering with organizations to understand their business objectives before identifying professionals who genuinely align with their vision and culture.

A Duality of Expertise

My approach to recruitment is shaped by two complementary disciplines — Strategic Human Resources and Behavioral Science.
Alongside over two decades of HR leadership experience, my qualifications as a Certified Counsellor and Psychometric Assessment Professional provide a deeper understanding of the people behind every resume. Together, they create a recruitment philosophy that values long-term fit as much as professional capability.

Sweety Vasani

Founder | Lakeside Recruit
