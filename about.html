Recruitment Beyond Resumes

Why is recruitment so often measured by the speed of hiring, rather than the long-term success of the people being hired?

With over two decades in Human Resoures, one belief has remained constant that recruitment should never be reduced to filling vacancies. Successful hiring extends far beyond matching resumes with job descriptions. Every hiring decision shapes culture, leadership, business performance, and the future of an organization.

Lakeside Recruit was established to provide organizations with a more strategic approach to talent acquisition. Rather than simply sourcing candidates, we partner with businesses to understand their goals, culture, workforce priorities, and long-term vision before recommending talent that genuinely aligns with those objectives.

A Duality of Expertise

This approach is shaped by a duality of Sweety Vasani's background. Over two decades of HR leadership has provided her deep understanding of Talent Strategy, Organizational Development, and Executive Hiring. Complementing her experience are her qualifications as a Certified Counsellor and Psychometric Assessment Professional. That combination changes how a search is run. A resume tells you what someone has done; a trained understanding of behaviour, motivation, and psychometric fit tells you whether they'll actually thrive in the role you're hiring for.

Lakeside Recruit is built on that same synthesis — the structure of strategic HR practice, informed by the psychological and assessment expertise to recognize genuine fit, not just qualification. bringing an additional behavioural perspective to recruitment as an optional premium assessment service providing an additional layer of confidence for leadership, specialist, and business-critical hiring decisions.
