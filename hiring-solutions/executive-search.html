WHAT'S AT STAKE

The Business Impact of Leadership Gaps

Director and VP-level roles set team direction, protect execution speed, and carry institutional knowledge that's hard to recover when the wrong hire is made.

Execution slowdown

A vacant or mis-hired functional head stalls team output and cross-department delivery.

Team instability

Senior individual contributors look upward for direction. Whereas the wrong leader unsettles entire functions.

Extended vacancy cost

Every week a senior role remains unfilled has a compounding cost on output and morale.

Culture & fit risk

Mid-leadership hires shape subcultures within teams. Whereas a poor fit spreads fast downward.
