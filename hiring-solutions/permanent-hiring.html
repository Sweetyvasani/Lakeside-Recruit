WHAT'S AT STAKE

A wrong permanent hire has a long tail.

Unlike contract roles, permanent hires carry probation periods, notice periods, and cultural impact. Hence, getting it right the first time matters more than getting it fast.

Cost of a mis-hire

Replacement searches, severance, and lost productivity compound quickly on a failed permanent placement.

Early attrition

Poor fit reveals itself within months — and restarts the entire hiring cycle at cost.

Team disruption

An unsettled hire affects team dynamics and productivity long before an exit happens.

Delayed Productivity

Every restart delays the business outcome the role was hired to deliver.
