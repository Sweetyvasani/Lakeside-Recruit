WHAT'S AT STAKE

Running recruitment in-house at scale has a hidden cost

When hiring volumes rise, internal HR teams stretch thin — quality of hire drops, process consistency breaks down, and the cost of running an in-house TA function starts to outweigh its value.

Inconsistent Process

Without a dedicated team, hiring quality varies by manager, function, and urgency — making outcomes unpredictable.

Internal Team Overload

HR teams managing day-to-day operations alongside recruitment volume can't do either well.

Rising Cost-per-hire

Multiple agency retainers, job board subscriptions, and internal hours add up quickly without a managed approach.

Scaling Bottleneck

Business growth stalls when hiring can't keep pace. An unmanaged TA function becomes a ceiling, not a support.

WHEN TO ENGAGE US

When should you consider RPO?

RPO works best when hiring is a sustained need, not a one-off requirement.

Your organization is in a sustained or rapid hiring phase
Your HR team is stretched and hiring quality is slipping
You're expanding into new locations, business units or markets.
Multiple open roles across functions need managing simultaneously
You want to reduce cost-per-hire without sacrificing quality

Without RPO

  • Fragmented, inconsistent process
  • Multiple agencies, rising cost
  • Internal team stretched thin
  • No visibility on hiring pipeline

With Lakeside Recruit RPO

  • Consistent process across all roles
  • Single partner, managed cost
  • HR team freed for strategic work
  • Full pipeline visibility & reporting
