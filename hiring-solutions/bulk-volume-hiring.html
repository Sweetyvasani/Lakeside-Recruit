WHAT'S AT STAKE

Volume without structure is just organised chaos.

When headcount targets are large, small inconsistencies in screening and selection multiply leading to high early attrition, quality variance across the batch, and a second wave of hiring before the first is even settled.

Quality inconsistency

Without a structured screening framework, quality of hire varies wildly across a large batch creating uneven teams from day one.

Early attrition at scale

Poor fit at volume means attrition compounds losing 20% of a large cohort early restarts a significant hiring and onboarding cost.

Timeline pressure

Business launches, project ramp-ups, and seasonal cycles don't move the workforce needs to be ready on time, every time.

HR Team Overload

HR and hiring manager bandwidth gets consumed entirely by volume hiring leaving no capacity for strategic or specialist roles running in parallel.
